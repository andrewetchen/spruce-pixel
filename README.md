## What a Shopify Storefront Audit Covers

Each store is evaluated across nine areas of UX that directly affect how visitors browse, trust, and buy:

- **Buyer experience and conversion paths** — Cart, checkout, and the full purchase flow
- **Navigation architecture** — Menus, information hierarchy, and wayfinding
- **Mobile responsiveness** — Touch targets, layout behavior, and small-screen performance
- **Visual design and brand trust** — Typography, imagery, consistency, and credibility signals
- **Product page effectiveness** — Galleries, descriptions, reviews, and variant selection
- **Cart and checkout flow** — Friction points, policy visibility, and payment clarity
- **Content hierarchy** — How information is structured across pages
- **Site-wide consistency** — Patterns, spacing, and interaction behavior across the store
- **Accessibility** — Color contrast, focus states, screen reader considerations

## What You Get

Every audit delivers prioritized findings (high, medium, low impact), annotated screenshots from your actual store, research citations for each recommendation, and clear action steps your team can hand directly to a developer. The final deliverable is a professionally designed PDF.
