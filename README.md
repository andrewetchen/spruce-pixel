# Spruce Pixel — Shopify Storefront UX Audits

We deliver expert [Shopify storefront UX audits](https://sprucepixel.com/pages/storefront-audit) built on structured heuristic analysis and grounded in e-commerce research from Baymard Institute, Nielsen Norman Group, and Google.
Every audit is conducted by former Shopify employees with hands-on experience across theme development, technical support, and UX evaluation for Plus merchants.

## What We Audit

Each storefront is evaluated across nine interconnected areas of UX:

- Buyer experience and conversion paths
- Navigation architecture
- Mobile responsiveness
- Visual design and brand trust
- Product page effectiveness
- Cart and checkout flow
- Content hierarchy
- Site-wide consistency
- Accessibility

Findings are prioritized by impact (high, medium, low), paired with annotated screenshots from your actual store, and delivered as a professionally designed PDF with clear action steps.
